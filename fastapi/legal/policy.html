Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 21.10.2024
Last Updated: 21.10.2024

LU Schedule ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app. Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms, do not use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • University Login Credentials: Used to authenticate and fetch schedules and exam data. These credentials are securely stored and not shared.
  • Cookies from University Website: Retrieved during login to maintain your session and fetch data on your behalf.
  • User Notes: Stored in the app for your personal use, including support for markdown (.md) formatting.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your login to the university's website.
  • Fetch schedules, exams, and other academic data.
  • Store user-generated notes for personal organization.
  • Improve the app's functionality and user experience.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Your university login credentials and cookies are used solely for their intended purpose and are not stored beyond what is necessary for the app's functionality.

4. Data Retention

  • University Credentials and Cookies: Retained for the duration of your app session and securely deleted when no longer needed.
  • User Notes: Retained until you delete them from the app.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and view the data stored in the app.
  • Request the deletion of your data at any time.
  • Withdraw your consent to data processing by discontinuing app usage.

6. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and secure storage mechanisms to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
